Introduction

The “See the Ability” advocacy highlights the strengths and talents of individuals with

disabilities. It creates an inclusive platform that shifts the focus away from limitations, encouraging the community to recognize and celebrate the abilities of everyone.

The goal is to empower PWDs to lead fulfilling lives, achieve their goals,

and make meaningful contributions to society.







Call for Action

The campaign invites community


members to participate by attending, supporting, or sharing the event. By

promoting the message of inclusion and celebrating the talents of individuals with disabilities, everyone can help

create a more accepting and accessible community.

Thank you for listening!
